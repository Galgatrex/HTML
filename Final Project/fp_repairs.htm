With hundreds of different types of equipment involved in our daily lives, it shouldn't come as much of a shock to learn that our lives can get pretty complicated!

From fuses to internal components, we must learn to troubleshoot to find the root of the problem, and then decide if we will require parts, or if we can fix the item on the spot.

I would say that a good 50% of calls I get are from incorrect use of equipment. It is always a good idea to read your manual! I have literally had to tell customers that their equipment won't work unless you plug it in first. That is a hard sentence to say when looking them in the eyes. Take a look at the tips page for some ideas on what to check for.
