BMETs use an assortment of standard and specialized tools to complete the work in their day to day jobs.

Most equipment can be repaired by using your standard house hold tools. In order to test the equipment for proper operation, you may need some specialized test tools, though.

The biggest part of our job is ensuring the accuracy of equipment that is used to measure aspects of your current health. You wouldn't want to get your blood pressure taken, not knowing if the results were accurate.

An incorrect measurement from medical equipment could lead to additional testing or for treatment for an illness that was never there to start with.
